Secure, end-to-end encrypted, and privacy respecting note taking app.

In order to use this application you need to have an account with EteSync (paid hosting), or run your own instance (free and open source). Check out https://www.etesync.com/ for more information.


Easy to Use
===========
EteSync is very easy to use. It's as simple as any of your non-encrypted note taking apps. Security doesn't always have to come at a cost.

Secure & Open
============
Thanks to zero-knowledge end-to-end encryption, not even we can see your data. Don't believe us? You shouldn't, just verify yourself, both the client and server are open source.

Full History
=========
A full history of your data is saved in an encrypted change history which means you can review, replay and revert any changes you have made at any point in time.
